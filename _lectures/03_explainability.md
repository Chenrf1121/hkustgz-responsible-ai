**Topics Covered:**
- Interpretability vs. explainability
- Inherently interpretable models: linear models, decision trees, rule-based systems
- Post-hoc explanation methods: LIME, SHAP, attention mechanisms
- Counterfactual explanations
- Evaluation of explanations

**Suggested Readings:**
- [Rudin, "Stop Explaining Black Box Models"](https://arxiv.org/abs/1811.10154)
- [Ribeiro et al., "Why Should I Trust You?"](https://arxiv.org/abs/1602.04938)
- [Lundberg & Lee, "A Unified Approach to Interpreting Model Predictions"](https://arxiv.org/abs/1705.07874)
